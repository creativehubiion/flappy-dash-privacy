Flappy Dash ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our mobile application.

Our app uses third-party advertising services (ironSource/Unity LevelPlay) to display ads. These services may collect and use certain information about your device and ad interactions, including:

- Device identifiers (Advertising ID, Device ID)
- IP address
- Device type and operating system
- Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

### Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our app, including:

- App usage statistics
- Crash reports
- Performance data

The information collected is used to:
